Biblyx — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Biblyx ("the app", "we", "us") is built and operated by an individual developer (not a legal entity), based in Azerbaijan. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the app, including international and European Union users, and explains what data is collected, how it is used, who it may be shared with, and your rights.

2. Data Controller

Data Controller: Galbinur, an individual developer residing in Azerbaijan (not a registered company).
Contact: biblyx.support@gmail.com

All privacy-related requests, including the rights listed below, can be sent to this address.

3. Data We Collect

  • Account data: via Supabase Auth, your email (if you signed up with email/password or Google) and a unique user ID. For anonymous use, only an anonymous device session identifier is stored.
  • Cloud Sync data (only if you sign in): your app settings, WordLab (your saved words), book metadata (title, author, reading progress), bookmarks and highlights. The book file itself is never sent off your device — it stays local.
  • AI translation data: the word/sentence/paragraph text you translate is sent to OpenAI through a secure server function (Supabase Edge Function) to produce a translation/analysis result. This text is processed under OpenAI's applicable data-processing policies and is not permanently stored by us. Biblyx does not use this text to train AI models.
  • Usage statistics (Firebase Analytics): anonymous counts and categories of which features are used. Book titles, looked-up words, or personal text are never sent to analytics. Depending on platform and settings, Firebase may automatically collect some additional technical interaction and diagnostic data.
  • Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics): if the app crashes unexpectedly, technical diagnostic data is sent automatically so we can find and fix issues.
  • Abuse-prevention identifier: to prevent abuse of the daily free AI quota (e.g. signing out to obtain a new free quota), a device-generated identifier is processed together with a daily usage counter, solely for abuse and quota-fraud prevention. It is not used for advertising, profiling, or tracking, and is not linked to your personal profile.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (for European Union users)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area, our processing of your data relies on the following legal bases:

  • Consent — when you create an account and use the AI translation feature;
  • Performance of a contract — to provide Cloud Sync and account features;
  • Legitimate interest — for app security, error detection, and anonymous usage statistics.

You may withdraw your consent at any time (see Section 8, Your Rights).

5. Third Parties and International Transfers

The app uses the following service providers. Each processes data under its own Privacy Policy, and your data may be stored on servers located outside Azerbaijan/the EU (e.g. the United States):

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and cloud sync. supabase.com/privacy
  • OpenAI — AI-based processing of translation/analysis requests. openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
  • Firebase / Google — analytics, crash reporting, and Google Sign-In. policies.google.com/privacy

We do not directly control these third parties' data-handling practices; we recommend reviewing their policies.

6. Local-First Principle

Your book files (PDF, EPUB, etc.) are stored only on your device and are never uploaded to our servers. Only metadata about the book (title, reading progress, etc.) is synced — never the book's content.

7. Data Retention

Your account data is kept for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all account and sync data is deleted immediately and permanently (in-app: Account → Delete my account). The daily abuse-prevention counter (Section 3) is recorded separately per calendar date, is not linked to your personal profile, and is not accumulated into a long-term usage history tied to your identity.

8. Your Rights

Whether you live in Azerbaijan or in the European Union (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • The right to access your data
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") — available instantly in-app via Account → Delete my account, or by requesting it at biblyx.support@gmail.com
  • The right to data portability (export)
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time
  • The right to object to processing

To exercise these rights: biblyx.support@gmail.com. EU residents also have the right to lodge a complaint with their national data protection supervisory authority.

9. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 (US, COPPA) or under the higher minimum digital consent age that applies in your country (up to 16 in some European countries), and we do not knowingly collect data from such individuals.

10. Security

We apply industry-standard technical measures (encrypted transmission, authentication) to protect your data, but no system transmitted over the internet can be guaranteed 100% secure.

11. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. You will be notified in-app of significant changes.

12. Contact

For questions: biblyx.support@gmail.com
This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with applicable privacy laws, including GDPR requirements for users in the European Economic Area, and has been prepared with the laws of the Republic of Azerbaijan in mind.
